Ten Brushes

A script to assign brush presets to any of ten configurable hotkeys.

Basic Usage

Go to Tools → Scripts → Shortcut Colors.

A Window will pop up with a set of boxes. Underneath the boxes is the hotkey the box is associated with.

Select a color from your palette and click one of the ten boxes to set it.

To change the associated hotkey, go to Settings → Configure Krita → Keyboard Shortcuts and then change the Activate Color actions under Ten Color

When done, open a file, and press your hotkeys to switch to that preset!
